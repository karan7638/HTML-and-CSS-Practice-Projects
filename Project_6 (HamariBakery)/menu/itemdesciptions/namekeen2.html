Garlic Bhel 400g

Garlic Bhel Namkeen is a crispy, spicy, and flavorful Indian snack, perfect for tea time. It combines puffed rice, roasted peanuts, and crunchy sev, infused with garlic, spices, and tangy flavors for a delicious munch.

Knife : Free

Candles : Free

Rs : 150.00

Weight : 400gm

Garlic Bhel – Quick Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • ½ cup roasted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil + ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder + salt to taste
  • ¼ cup sev (for extra crunch)✨
